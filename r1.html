Analysis of camera movement and observations

As per the suggestion of the reviewer, we visualize the behavior of the camera movement and corresponding depth and rgb (only for visualization) observations from the robot's camera on the bottom right of each video. As shown in the videos, the actuated camera allows the robot to attend to the most important information of the environment with respect to the heading direction of the robot and nearby obstacles at any given time. Note that the arm not only moves to avoid obstacles but also to minimize any occlusions to the camera while moving.
