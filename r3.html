Generalization to non-flat surfaces

Even though we only use convex and flat surfaced cuboids of different dimensions during phase1 training, our final distilled depth-conditioned policy is robust to different shaped objects. This can also be seen from our real-world videos which have a wide variety of obstacles as well as distractor objects. Below we show robustness to two such shapes -- sphere (left) and capsule (right) obstacles in simulation. The videos are overlayed with corresponding rgb and depth images on the top right for analysis. Note that the policy only operates on depth images and rgb is only included for visualization.
